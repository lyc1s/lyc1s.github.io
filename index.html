Homogeneous Volume Rendering for Subsurface Scattering

For our project we plan to implement subsurface scattering for homogeneous volumes. In order to do so, we will build on our current path tracer implementation to support ray transmission using a BTDF component. At each intersection, we now have the probability of generating either a reflected ray or a refracted ray. We will use multiple importance sampling in order to decide whether the BTDF or BRDF should be sampled. Additionally, we will take into consideration the effects of scattering and absorption along each ray. In order to display the effects of subsurface scattering, we will be rendering a ball of wax with one light source in the background.
